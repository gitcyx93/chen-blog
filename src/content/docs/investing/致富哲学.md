### Wealth & Cash Flow
- The key takeaway: **Prioritize the [[cash flow]] of psychological energy**.  
  - 总结就是重视心理能量的现金流。  
- In the early stages, **cut costs wherever possible** and **make do with what you have**. The key is to maintain a **positive cash flow cycle**.  
  - 起步阶段，怎么减成本怎么来，怎么将就怎么来，关键是现金流的正循环。  

### Wealth-Building Principles
- The opportunity to get rich does not have *three-strike outs*. If you're unsure, **you don't have to swing**.  
  - 致富的机会没有 *三振出局*。没有把握的时候可以不用挥杆。  
- **Owning a private business** is the necessary path to financial freedom.  
  - 拥有私人生意是通向财富自由的必经之路。  
- *No one is crazy.* Don't judge others' choices based on your own experiences.  
  - *no one is crazy*，不要用你的经历去衡量别人的选择。  
- **Never being satisfied makes rich people do stupid things**.  
  - 永不满足，会让富人办傻事。  
  - If your goals keep rising with your current situation, *when will you ever be satisfied?*  
    - 如果你的目标随着你的现状一直在提高，*那么什么时候你才能满足呢？*  
- [[Reasonable > Rational]]– Sometimes, a **reasonable decision** is better than a *cold, purely rational* one.  
  - **reasonable > rational**，情理上讲得通的决定有时候比 *冰冷的* 绝对理性的决定更好。  
  - **Sustained motivation comes from continuous positive feedback**.  
    - **持续的动力来自持续的正反馈**。
